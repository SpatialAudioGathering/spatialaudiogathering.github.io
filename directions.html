Directions


Spatial Audio Gathering 2026 takes place at the Leeds Beckett School of Arts, Portland Way, Leeds LS1.

Getting here

The closest airport is Leeds Bradford Airport, however Manchester airport is an hour and a half away via train (Manchester airport - Manchester Picadilly, Manchester Victoria - Leeds) and offers a larger range of international flights.


The Leeds School of Arts building is a 15 minute walk from the Leeds train station.





If travelling by coach, the walk to the LSA is 20 minutes


Staying in Leeds


The closest budget hotel is the City Centre Premier Inn (approximately 10-minute walk from the LSA).




Other options within walking distance include the Radisson Blu, Travelodge, and Ibis Style.

Printing Posters

If you are printing a poster, we suggest using Ryman's online printing facilities.


Once you have selected preferences (A3 on 90g+ paper) you can order click and collect to the Leeds branch (17 The Merrion Centre Leeds, LS2 8NG)


Ryman's will email you once ready. Then simply pick it up following the map (A 10-minute walk from the LSA approximately).



As poster sessions are exhibited all day, we require posters to be ready by 9am the morning of presentation.






If you have any further travel questions, please email us at sag.conf@gmail.com
